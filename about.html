Our Story

Built by Authors,
for Authors.

Inkbound was born out of frustration. Our founder, James Calloway, spent three years trying to publish his first book — navigating predatory vanity presses, indifferent literary agents, and confusing self-publishing platforms that left him with a poorly designed paperback and zero marketing support.

So he built what he wished existed: a publishing agency that combines the editorial quality of a traditional publisher with the author-friendly economics of self-publishing. Where you keep your rights, keep your royalties, and still get a team that treats your book like it matters.

Twelve years later, that idea has grown into a 48-person team of editors, designers, marketers, and publishing strategists — all of them devoted to one thing: making sure your story reaches the people who need to read it.

"The best publishing deal is one where the author wins. That's been our north star since day one."
JC
James Calloway Founder & CEO, Inkbound Publishing
